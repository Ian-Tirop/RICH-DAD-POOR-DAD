Summary of Rich Dad Poor Dad

Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki explores contrasting financial philosophies between the author's two father figures: his biological father ("Poor Dad") and the father of his best friend ("Rich Dad"). The book emphasizes the importance of financial education, understanding assets vs. liabilities, and the value of entrepreneurship. It challenges traditional beliefs about money and encourages readers to think differently about earning and investing.
